Our love story

First met

Dec 20, 2016

Andrew and Emma first met in 2017 at a Christmas party that their church was having for the college and career students. There were quite a few people at the party and Emma had also brought her best friend along with her. They hadn’t ever spoken before but had seen each other around church before. Emma thought he was cute and Andrew said he first noticed her blue eyes from across the room. From then on they messaged each other on Facebook and have together ever since.

First date

Feb 14 2017

Andrew and Emma first started dating in January 2017 but their first official date was that year on Valentine’s Day. They both were nervous but Emma had a special day for them planned. They took a trip to Cedar Hill State Park which was one of Emma’s favorite places as a kid. She had brought everything for a perfect picnic together. After they ate, they went for a walk into Penn Farm and wandered around until their feet hurt. It was a very memorable moment for the both of them.

Proposal

Aug 26 2018

On August 26, 2018 Andrew William Chumchal proposed to Emma Kate Shields. It was at Cedar Hill State Park at the Penn Farm. Both families were there and ran out after Emma had shouted yes! It was more amazing than anything she had ever imagined.

Enagagement

Dec 08 2018

Engagement life has treated Andrew and Emma pretty well. Of course there have been fights and arguments, but they know that prayer and communication are needed in order to work things out for the better. .They are both working hard to be where they want in life and will continue to love each other no matter what.
